# Refined Jiji - Go-to-Market Strategy

## Executive Summary

Refined Jiji is a modern, AI-powered classifieds platform designed to disrupt the African marketplace. With superior user experience, smart recommendations, and trust & safety features, Refined Jiji targets young professionals seeking quality items in urban Nigeria.

**Launch Timeline**: 8 weeks from beta to full launch
**Target Market**: 25-40 year olds in Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt
**Key Differentiators**: AI recommendations, modern UX, escrow payments

## Market Analysis

### Market Size & Opportunity
- **Nigerian Classifieds Market**: $2.5B annual GMV
- **Mobile Commerce Growth**: 300% YoY growth
- **Young Professional Segment**: 15M potential users
- **Current Market Leader**: Jiji.ng (70% market share)
- **Opportunity**: 25% market share within 18 months

### Competitive Landscape
- **Jiji.ng**: Legacy platform, cluttered UX, basic features
- **OLX**: International player, less localized
- **Facebook Marketplace**: Social features, less transactional
- **Craigslist-style locals**: Fragmented, no trust features

### Competitive Advantages
- **AI-Powered**: Smart recommendations vs basic search
- **Modern UX**: Clean design vs cluttered interfaces
- **Trust & Safety**: Verification and escrow vs risky transactions
- **Mobile-First**: iOS app priority vs web-centric approach
- **Local Focus**: Nigerian market expertise vs generic platforms
