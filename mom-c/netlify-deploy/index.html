A Mother's Journey

"They didn't just give me medicine. They gave me hope."

When Sarah found out she was pregnant, she was alone and without resources. She feared for her future and the health of her child.

Through Mom-C's Maternal Care program, she received consistent prenatal checkups, nutritional support, and a community of women who stood by her. Today, her son is thriving, and Sarah is training to become a community health advocate.
